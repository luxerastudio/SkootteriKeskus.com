4. VAT (ALV) and pricing transparency

Where applicable, prices are shown and invoices issued in line with Finnish VAT rules. The standard VAT rate in Finland is currently 25.5% (ALV). Example:

  • Net price (excl. VAT): €100.00
  • VAT (25.5%): €25.50
  • Gross total (incl. VAT): €125.50

Payments may be processed by Stripe and/or PayPal. They act as independent processors under their own privacy notices. We only receive the information needed to confirm payment and fulfil your order.

Where data is transferred outside the European Economic Area, we rely on appropriate safeguards under GDPR (e.g. Standard Contractual Clauses) as offered by our processors.
