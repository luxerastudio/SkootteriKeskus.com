Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · SkootteriKeskus Oy · Finland

1. Scope

This policy explains returns, refunds, and the statutory 14-day withdrawal right for distance contracts under EU consumer protection rules, as implemented in Finland, where applicable.

2. Right of withdrawal (14 days)

If you are a consumer and you bought online or otherwise at distance, you generally have 14 days to withdraw from the contract without giving a reason, starting from the day you (or a third party you designate) receive the goods — unless an exception applies (see below).

To exercise the right of withdrawal, notify us clearly before the deadline (e.g. email or written message) with your name, order reference, and decision to withdraw.

3. Exceptions

Some products are exempt from withdrawal or have special rules (e.g. sealed goods opened for hygiene reasons, custom-made items, certain digital content after delivery, or goods that cannot be returned due to nature). We will inform you if an exception applies to your order.

4. Returns & condition

Returned goods must be unused, in original packaging where reasonable, and include all accessories unless otherwise agreed. You are liable for any diminished value only if handling went beyond what is necessary to establish the nature and characteristics of the goods.

5. Refunds & VAT (ALV)

If you validly withdraw, we refund all payments received from you, including delivery costs at the basic rate (if you were charged), without undue delay and within 14 days from when we receive your withdrawal notice or the goods, whichever is later — unless we have offered to collect the goods.

Refunds are calculated consistently with how VAT (ALV) was charged on the original sale. With the current standard Finnish VAT rate of 25.5%, a simple illustration:

  • Paid total (incl. 25.5% VAT): €125.50
  • Net portion ≈ €100.00 · VAT portion ≈ €25.50

Exact refund lines on your statement may vary depending on payment provider (Stripe/PayPal) processing.

6. Payment security

Refunds are issued through the same payment channel where possible. Card data is handled by PCI-DSS compliant providers (e.g. Stripe, PayPal); we do not store your full card details.

7. Warranty claims

Defective goods may be subject to statutory warranty rights separate from withdrawal. Contact us with photos and order details for assessment.
